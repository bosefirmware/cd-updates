Lifestyle 18, 28 & 35 Systems
How to Check the Software/Firmware Revision  
  
Viewed on AV28 console

- Turn the media center off.
- On the console press and hold the STORE button and then press the ENTER button... 
- For each additional press of the ENTER button, you will get additional information. 
  See the list below. 

01 - House Code: (code)

02 - SW C: (version)
  
03 - SN (s/n)

04 - SW1A: (version)

05 - DVD Region Code: (#)
  
06 - DVD FIRMWARE: (version) 
  
07 - Tuner Board: (version)
  
08 - IR Blaster: (version)
