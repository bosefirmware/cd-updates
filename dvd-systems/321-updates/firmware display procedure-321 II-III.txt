Firmware display information procedure
---------------------------------------

Viewed on AV321II    (321 II & 321GS II)  console displays Steps 1 - 16
Viewed on AV321IIGSX     (321 GSX)        console displays Steps 1 - 20
Viewed on AV321IIGSXL    (321 GSXL)       console displays Steps 1 - 20


Viewed on AV321III   (321 III & 321GS III)  console displays Steps 1 - 16, 20-22
Viewed on AV321IIIGSX     (321GSX III)      console displays Steps 1 - 22
Viewed on AV321IIIGSXL    (321GSXL III)     console displays Steps 1 - 22

Obtaining System Information from the Media Center Display
- Turn the media center off.
- On the console press and hold the ENTER/STORE button on the media center.
- While holding down the ENTER/STORE button, each additional press of the
  POWER button will show additional information. See the list below.
- Once you are done viewing the information, release the ENTER/STORE button.
  If information is still being displayed press the POWER button.

01 - Video output: NTSC  Progressive ->  NTSC  Component ->  NTSC  Composite+S ->
                  *MULTI Progressive -> *MULTI Component -> *MULTI Composite+S ->
                  *PAL   Progressive ->  PAL Composite+S ->  PAL   Component   ->
                  [Use the volume buttons to cycle through options]
                  (If HDMI used only "NTSC" or "PAL" is displayed)

xx - *Hotel Control: (off -> on) (Available only on AV321II & AV321III)
                     [Use the volume buttons to cycle through options]
                     (Max volume is set to the current volume setting)

02 - Flash Commit [press volume up twice to save settings and reboot]
03 - Console Software: (version)
04 - Console Variant: (#) (if an American system 129=AV321II/AV321III & 134=AV321GSX/AV321GSXIII)
05 - Bass Box Software: (version)
06 - **Bass Box EQ: (#)
07 - Tuner Software: (version)
08 - Console serial number: (s/n)
09 - **Bass Serial number: (s/n)
10 - Main Board serial number: (s/n)
11 - Tuner serial number: (s/n)
12 - DVD Region Code: (#)
13 - DVD Drive: (firmware version on first line)
                (DVD model number on second line)
14 - Main Board Functional: (test results)
15 - Tuner Board Alignment: (test results)
16 - Serial Port: (Smart Speaker -> ETAP)
                  [Use the volume buttons to cycle through options]
17 - Hard Drive: (firmware version on first line)
                 (DVD model number on second line)
18 - Hard Drive Self Test: (test results)
19 - Gracenote Database: (version)

xx - Ethernet Address: (Address) (Available only on 321GSX series II systems)

20 - ***DRC Timing: #x##
21 - ****HDMIC: (#)
22 - ****SRM (#)

*    Included with Console firmware 02.01.00 and greater
**   Included with Console firmware 02.02.00 and greater
***  Included with Console firmware 03.01.00 and greater
**** Included with 321 series III systems (CN 04.01.00 +)
